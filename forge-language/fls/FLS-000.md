# FLS-000: Constitution

Status: Draft Baseline
Version: FLS-0
Category: Governance
Normative Level: Level A

## Purpose
Defines the governance and constitutional principles of Forge.

## Scope
Defines governance, interpretation rules, constitutional principles, and conformance philosophy.

## Core Requirements
- FLS-000-CNF-001: Implementations claiming conformance SHALL identify the FLS version and maturity level.
- FLS-000-CNF-002: Normative behavior SHALL NOT be established outside the RFC process.
- FLS-000-CNF-003: Where implementation behavior conflicts with the FLS, the FLS SHALL take precedence.
